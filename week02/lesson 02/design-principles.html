Design Principles Document

Visual hierarchy

Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance

Rule of Thirds

The rule of thirds is a composition guideline that places your subject in the left or right third of an image, leaving the other two thirds more open

Fitts's Law

Fitts's Law describes how long it takes a user to hit a target in a graphical user interface (GUI) or other design, as a function of size and distance
